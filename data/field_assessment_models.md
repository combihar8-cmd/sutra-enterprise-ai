# Field Assessment Models & Planning Framework

---

# 1. Purpose of Field Assessment

Field assessment is the process of collecting real-world information from project locations, client sites, villages, institutions, businesses, industries, government bodies, or operational areas to:

* Understand ground realities
* Identify problems and opportunities
* Evaluate feasibility
* Measure risks
* Validate project assumptions
* Build implementation strategy
* Estimate cost, manpower, and timeline
* Generate actionable reports

This framework can be used for:

* Infrastructure Projects
* Rural Development Projects
* CSR Projects
* Startup Consulting
* Skill Development Programs
* Government Projects
* NGO Projects
* Industrial Surveys
* Market Research
* Technology Deployment
* Real Estate Assessment
* Environmental Assessment
* Social Impact Studies

# 2. Core Field Assessment Models

## Model 1 — Rapid Assessment Model (RAM)

### Objective

Quick understanding of a location/project within 1–3 days.

### Used For

* Initial client meetings
* Quick feasibility checks
* Emergency assessments
* Preliminary investment decisions

### Process

1. Site Visit
2. Stakeholder Interaction
3. Photo & Video Documentation
4. Basic Data Collection
5. Risk Identification
6. Summary Report

### Deliverables

* Quick Assessment Report
* Site Images
* Initial Risk Notes
* Opportunity Matrix

### Timeline

1–3 Days

### Team Requirement

* 1 Field Executive
* 1 Technical Advisor

---

## Model 2 — Detailed Feasibility Assessment Model (DFAM)

### Objective

Comprehensive technical and operational analysis before project execution.

### Used For

* Large Projects
* Government Tenders
* Infrastructure Projects
* Investment Planning
* Industrial Setup

### Assessment Areas

* Technical Feasibility
* Financial Feasibility
* Operational Feasibility
* Legal Feasibility
* Environmental Feasibility
* Social Feasibility

### Data Collection Methods

* GPS Mapping
* Drone Survey
* Interviews
* Structured Questionnaires
* Focus Group Discussion
* Document Review
* Ground Measurements

### Deliverables

* DPR (Detailed Project Report)
* Cost Estimation
* Risk Assessment
* SWOT Analysis
* Execution Roadmap

### Timeline

1–6 Weeks

### Team Requirement

* Project Manager
* Technical Experts
* Survey Team
* Data Analyst
* Financial Analyst
